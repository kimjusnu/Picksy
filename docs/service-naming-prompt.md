# Service Naming Prompt

You are a senior brand strategist and naming consultant for consumer internet products.

I am building a mobile-first web app where people tap between two relatable choices, then log in to see how many others picked the same side. The service mixes:

- quick voting
- curiosity about crowd behavior
- anonymous or semi-anonymous community energy
- repeat visits driven by new prompts, reactions, and identity
- Korean and global users together

The current product direction is similar to:

- choice battles
- lightweight community participation
- social curiosity
- retention loops

It can be inspired by products like Blind in terms of strong community habit and repeated checking behavior, but it must not feel like a copy or corporate-only service.

I want a name with layered meaning. It should feel like it can imply several of these ideas at once:

- choice
- signal
- echo
- crowd
- identity
- pulse
- split opinion
- hidden inner preference
- reaction
- connection

Requirements:

- suggest 30 names in total
- names should be short, strong, and brandable
- 1 to 3 syllables preferred
- easy to pronounce in both Korean and English
- should feel modern, memorable, and app-friendly
- should not sound too generic, childish, or overly corporate
- avoid names that are too close to MBTI, quiz, test, psychology clinic, survey tool, or HR platform branding
- avoid names that are obviously tied only to dating, food, or one category
- avoid names that are hard to spell after hearing once

For each name, output:

1. Name
2. Pronunciation
3. Core meanings
4. Why it fits this product
5. Tone keywords
6. Risk or downside

Then choose your best 5 names and for each of those provide:

- short tagline in Korean
- short tagline in English
- what kind of visual identity it suggests
- whether it feels better for Korea-first, global-first, or both

Finally:

- rank the top 3 overall
- explain which one is strongest if the goal is high retention and strong community identity
- explain which one is strongest if the goal is premium brand expansion later
